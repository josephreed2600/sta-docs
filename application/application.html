For current 5th graders: Our Fall enrollment period runs from Sept.1 to Nov 30, and our Spring enrollment period runs from April 1 to April 30 for the following school year. If we do not fill the class during an enrollment period, all applicants will be admitted. If the class fills, all applicants in that enrollment period will be entered into a public lottery to determine position on the waiting list.

For current 6th, 7th, and 8th graders: Our Spring enrollment period runs from April 1 to April 30 for the following school year. All applicants will be entered into a public lottery to determine position on the waiting list. Applicants currently on a waiting list will be included in the lottery, so positions may shift on the waiting list from year to year. All students who submit an application after April 30 will be added to the waiting list in the order received.

All completed applications must be submitted by 3:00 pm on November 30 (fall enrollment period) and April 30 (spring enrollment period).

 

Three Part Application Process

¨ Complete application (attached)

¨ Student essay

¨ Skills Inventory Test

 

Application: Please complete all sections of the attached application. We will use this information to contact you about your enrollment status.

Student Essay: Students, please write a short essay (maximum one page) sharing up to three careers you are interested in, and how a STEM education would support your     interests.

Skills Inventory Test: This test covers basic skills in Math, English, Science, and Critical Thinking. Upon submission of the Application and Student Essay, register for a testing session. Once the test is completed, it will be signed by the student and sealed. We will not score it until your child is admitted to the school.
